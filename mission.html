Mission Secret Santa partners with our local homeless shelter to make sure every child wakes up to a gift chosen especially for them.

Donors can browse first names, ages, and wish lists, then send gifts straight to the shelter. We protect each child’s privacy while still sharing their unique interests and hopes for Christmas morning.

This project is built to be simple, safe, and scalable so more kids can feel seen and celebrated during the holidays.
